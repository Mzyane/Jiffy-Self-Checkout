Our Story

Jiffy is a proud Omani startup that began as a university project at the German University of Technology in Oman (GUtech). What started as a graduation project by a team of passionate students has evolved into a nationally recognized innovation, transforming the way people shop across Oman.

Our mission is to revolutionize the retail landscape by introducing cutting-edge smart checkout technology that eliminates long queues and creates seamless, frictionless shopping experiences. We are dedicated to empowering businesses with intelligent solutions that enhance customer satisfaction and drive growth for retailers.
