Why Choose Jiffy?

Our technology is designed to address the key challenges faced by modern retailers, helping you improve efficiency, cut costs, and enhance the customer experience.

Faster Checkout Flow

Eliminate long queues and reduce checkout times using our RFID self-checkout gateway. Customers pass through the gate and complete purchases in seconds—resulting in smoother operations and improved store throughput.

Lower Labor Costs

Automate the checkout process and reduce reliance on cashiers. With Jiffy, staff can focus on stocking, customer support, and improving store efficiency rather than scanning items manually.

Our Advanced technology ensures precise product detection and minimizes errors or missed scans. Directional antennas and controlled reading zones help reduce shrinkage and ensure secure transactions.
